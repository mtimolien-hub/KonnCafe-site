The Story

More Than
a Cup.

KonnCafe is a premium coffee brand rooted in Haitian cultural heritage, crafted in Connecticut with the finest Latin American beans. The name "Konn" draws from Haitian Creole, meaning "to know" — an invitation to discover bold flavor through cultural connection. Every cup is an experience: layered, intentional, and deeply rooted in story. Our mission is to connect cultures through exceptional coffee, honoring Haitian heritage while crafting a bold, modern American brand.
